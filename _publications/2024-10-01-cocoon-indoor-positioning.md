We present an integrated simulated annealing algorithm for WiFi-based indoor positioning using Channel State Information (CSI).

Key contributions:
- A simulated-annealing-based optimization framework for Time-of-Flight (ToF) indoor localization
- Combined triangulation and the MUSIC algorithm for transmitter localization
- A two-stage error mitigation pipeline including data cleaning and nonlinear optimization
- Demonstrated superior positioning accuracy and robustness compared with traditional optimization methods such as L-BFGS

This paper was accepted at the **30th International Computing and Combinatorics Conference (COCOON 2024)**, and received a **Competition Award** at the conference.
